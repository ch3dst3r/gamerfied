1.7 The internet is dramatically changing the way we communicate, send data, pay our bills etc. By entering our personal information online to make life that little bit easier.. Then you’re contacted by a company you have given your details to… and you are like ‘whoa!, you haven’t even bought me a drink yet!’.. but they say they are using your details to better your ‘customer experience’, can you really believe that, or do you wonder what they actually do with your information?. This is where GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) comes in, like a self made caped crusader looking for their first chance to impress.. They were enforced in may 2018 and changed the way business's collect and store customer information, and frighteningly, to GDPRs surprise not a lot of business’s know about them (come on GDPR, you’ve only just started!).. but now, most do! And GDPR is here to step in between you, your information and the business you are giving it to and say ‘Hey, before you do anything with that, buy the guy a drink!’.. so it’s like a business having a dinner date with you and GDPR is your secretly hidden away personal bodyguard, the one that lets you have a right to access your information, your information to be forgotten (in case the date goes bad!) and to have your information corrected etc. It’s either that or your dinner date could end up paying a bill worth millions.. now that would be an expensive garlic bread!.
